💎 Glamour Fun Facts!

Amazing things to know about fashion dolls and glamour!

👗
Fact #1

So Many Outfits!

Fashion dolls have worn over 1 billion tiny outfits since they were first created! That's more clothes than any real person could ever wear! 🤩

👩‍🚀
Fact #2

She's Been to Space!

Fashion dolls have had more than 200 different careers — including astronaut, doctor, president, and even a computer engineer! Girls can be anything! 🚀

🌍
Fact #3

World Famous!

Fashion dolls are sold in over 150 countries! If you lined them all up, they would circle the Earth more than 11 times. That is a LOT of dollies! 🌏

👠
Fact #4

Tiny Shoes!

Fashion dolls have tiny feet — about 3cm long! If you had the same proportions, your feet would be the size of a dinner plate! Fun to imagine! 👣

🎂
Fact #5

A Very Old Friend!

The very first fashion doll appeared in 1959 — that is over 65 years ago! Your grandma might have played with one when she was little too! 👵💖

🎨
Fact #6

Hair Colours!

Fashion dolls have had hair in almost every colour you can imagine — pink, blue, purple, rainbow, silver and even green! What colour would you choose? 🌈

👯
Fact #7

She Has Friends!

Fashion dolls have had over 40 different friends and family members! They even have sisters, cousins and a whole big group of friends to play with! 🥰

🏠
Fact #8

Dream Houses!

The famous Dreamhouse has had over 60 versions since 1962! Each one is bigger and more glamorous than the last — some even have working lifts and pools! 🏊

💗
Fact #9

Pink is Her Colour!

Glamour pink is one of the most recognised colours in the whole world! When people see bright pink, they often think of fashion, fun and fabulous dolls! 💕
